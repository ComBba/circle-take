# Circle Take - 3 Minute Demo Script

## Rule

The demo must stay under 3 minutes. The first 30 seconds must show the CUT ritual before explaining the system.

---

## 0:00-0:10 - Take 1 Failure

**Visual:** Clay stop-motion style scene. Luna the black cat hides a smartwatch ad. Her red ribbon is missing. The alarm clock face has become digital.

**On-screen text:**

> Take 1: something broke.

**Narration:**

> Luna lost her red ribbon. The alarm clock changed identity.

---

## 0:10-0:18 - CUT

**Visual:** The episode stops. A slate/clapper cuts in.

**On-screen text:**

> CUT.

**Narration:**

> Circle Take does not hide bad takes.

---

## 0:18-0:30 - Continuity Court

**Visual:** Qwen verdict JSON appears.

**On-screen JSON:**

```json
{
  "violation": "fixed_marker_missing",
  "actor": "Luna",
  "contract_broken": "red_ribbon",
  "prop_drift": "paper_dial_changed_to_digital",
  "repair_action": "reshoot_shot_2_only"
}
```

**Narration:**

> Qwen judges the take against Actor and Style Contracts. Only Shot 2 goes back for a Take Two.

---

## 0:30-0:50 - Product Explanation

**Visual:** Product title screen.

**On-screen text:**

> Circle Take  
> Bad takes don't make the cut.

**Narration:**

> Circle Take is a self-correcting production loop for generated episodes. It catches broken continuity, reshoots only the failed shot, and remembers only approved takes.

---

## 0:50-1:15 - Brief to Contracts

**Visual:** User brief -> Actor Contract / Style Contract / Story Contract cards.

**Narration:**

> The user starts with a short drama brief. Scripty turns it into contracts: who the actors are, what style rules must hold, and what the episode must communicate.

---

## 1:15-1:40 - Storyboard Slate and Shot Risk Ledger

**Visual:** 4 shot cards. Shot 2 is marked high risk.

**Narration:**

> Scripty builds a storyboard slate, then scores identity risk. Shot 2 is high risk because Luna's tail hides the ad and her ribbon can disappear.

---

## 1:40-2:05 - Generation Route

**Visual:** Route selector chooses R2V for Shot 2, T2V/R2V for other shots. Wan/HappyHorse task progress appears.

**Narration:**

> Circle Take does not use one generation route for every shot. Character-critical shots use references. Repairable failures use edit or regeneration routes.

---

## 2:05-2:35 - Review and Repair

**Visual:** Take 1 plays again. Continuity Court highlights missing ribbon and digital dial. Reshoot Spell appears.

**Narration:**

> The first take is reviewed, not trusted. Qwen returns a verdict, and Reshoot Spell creates a delta-only repair instruction for the failed shot.

---

## 2:35-2:50 - Take Two Reveal

**Visual:** Before/After comparison. Before: no ribbon and digital clock. After: ribbon restored and paper dial restored.

**Narration:**

> The rest of the episode is preserved. Only the broken shot gets a Take Two.

---

## 2:50-3:00 - Memory and Auto Greenlight

**Visual:** Memory wall with red thread. Approved anchor stored. Next cold open appears.

**On-screen text:**

> Only approved takes become memory.

**Narration:**

> Failed keyframes are quarantined. Approved anchors become memory. The next episode starts from what the show remembers.
